We take the protection of your personal data seriously. This privacy policy explains what data we collect when you use this website and how we handle it, in line with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (revFADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What data we collect

Waitlist sign-up: When you join our waitlist, we collect the email address you provide. We use it solely to inform you about the launch of the Lophi Timer and related updates.

Server data: When you visit this website, technical information (such as your browser type, approximate region and time of access) may be processed automatically by our hosting provider for security and stability purposes.

Email service provider

We use Mailchimp (Intuit Inc.) to manage our waitlist and send emails. The email address you submit is stored and processed on Mailchimp's servers. Mailchimp may process data outside Switzerland and the EU. For details, see Mailchimp's own privacy policy.

Legal basis and purpose

We process your email address based on your consent, which you give by submitting the sign-up form. The purpose is limited to keeping you informed about Lophi.

How long we keep your data

We keep your email address until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it, or until the purpose (informing you about the launch) no longer applies.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw your consent at any time. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. To exercise your rights, contact us at info@lophi.ch.

Cookies

This website uses only the technical cookies necessary for its basic functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy as our project develops. The current version always applies.
